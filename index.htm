AR Navigation is useful to find several locations in and around the campus. We are still building it and the work is under process. But once it is done, you'll be able to find all your day-to-day useful locations with a fun element of AR! The AR element will guide you through the way and will help you to reach your destination quickly and easily!

Check out how does it work and how to use the service through the navbar or by clicking on the three dots.
